Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how Funland ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Details: Including your name, email address, shipping address, and phone number when you register or make a purchase.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via a third-party provider. We do not store your full payment details on our servers.
  • Usage Data: Such as IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited to help us improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process orders and manage customer service interactions.
  • To send transactional and promotional communications (only if you opt-in).
  • To improve website functionality and personalize your user experience.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • Service Providers: We may share your data with partners who help us operate the site and fulfill services.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or to protect our rights.

4. Security of Your Information

We use a combination of technical and administrative safeguards to protect your data. Secure payment gateways are used for all transactions. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices & Rights

  • You may access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time by logging into your account or contacting us.
  • You can unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link provided in the messages.

6. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18. If we discover we’ve received information from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page, and material changes will be highlighted.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact Us page.
